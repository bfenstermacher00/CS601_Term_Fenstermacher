About Me

Hi there! My name is Brian and I'm a web designer currently working at WebFX and working towards a masters degree in software development. My path to development is a bit of an unconventional one, starting off with my batchelors of fine arts in graphic design, then moving into web design, shifting within my position to more of a frontend developer and designer hybrid and finally recognizing my interest in development.

I've found that my approach has become extremely valuable for this industry as it's taught me so much about all areas of the web. WebFX, my current employer, has set me on a terrific path that has allowed me to establish a deep knowledge of HTML, CSS and PHP, all of which I've found to be assets in the field of development.

On more of a personal level, I'm a huge outdoors person. I love running, biking, rock climbing and really anything that gets me outside. In 2020 I completed my first marathon and have done 2 since including one ultra marathon. Don't let my athletic hobbies fool you though, I'm still a huge enjoyer of food, coffee and good beer (the sour kind please).
