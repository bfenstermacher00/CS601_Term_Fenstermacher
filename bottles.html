Skandi Bottles

About The Project

I've been working with Meduseld Meadery as their graphic designer for roughly 6 years and while doing so, they were interested in branding out and creating a new brand called Skandi. For this, they were looking for a minimalist Skandinavian branding that would reflect the minimalist ingredients in their beverages. We landing on this design which featured a hand lettered type for the main brand and tied that to a serif font that emphasized their professionalism.

Key Features:

  • Hand lettered branding
  • Illustrations for the sweetness scale (mountain graphic)
  • Established brand standard to be used across flavor line
